Pocket Folders & Specialty Cartons

Among our many specialties at Fine Arts, we take pride in our repertoire of presentation folders, cartons and other novelty designs. Our presentation folders represent a gallery of creativity, expressed through a myriad of materials, colors and techniques. Sizes range from a double pocket folder that fits into a number ten envelope to a three-panel legal size folder. Most have business card slits. Some have windows, others file tabs, and much, much more. At Fine Arts we can foil stamp, emboss or finish in any way that fits your specific needs. Ask our expert sales staff for assistance in designing a folder you can be proud to show.

As an extension of our die cutting capabilities, we also produce cartons and sleeves for CD's, pop-ups and other product specialties. Let your imagination flow and you are sure to think of cartons that can be used as sales tools or point-of-purchase designs. Perhaps a tray and sleeve for notepads, or unusual cartons.

Like all specialty work, diecutting poses some hazards for the novice. As always, feel free to consult our experienced craftsman for assistance with selection of proper paper and with configuration and placement of the die. With your ingenuity and Fine Arts' expertise and equipment, we are sure to produce a quality product that works for you.
